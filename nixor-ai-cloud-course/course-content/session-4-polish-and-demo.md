# Session 4 — Polish, the Big Picture & Demo Day

**Goal:** finish a version you're proud of, understand the grown-up concerns, and present.

## Key ideas
- **Cost** — open Cost Analysis and see what your app *actually* cost to run. Real numbers.
- **Scale** — what happens if 100 people use your app at once? (A taste of scaling.)
- **Responsible AI** — content safety, privacy, and not trusting AI output blindly.
- **Infrastructure as Code** — everything in your sandbox was built by one script
  (`main.bicep`). That's how pros create identical environments repeatably.

## Steps
1. Polish: tighten your system prompt, improve the look, add one nice touch.
2. Look at your real spend in Cost Analysis. Discuss what drove it (tokens!).
3. Skim `infra/main.bicep` — see how *your whole sandbox* is described in one file.
4. **Demo day**: present your live app. What does it do? What did each Azure piece do?

## Where to go next
The AZ-900 (Azure Fundamentals) certification, and building your next app on what you now
know. You've deployed real AI software to the cloud — most adults never have.
